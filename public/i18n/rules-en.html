How to Play

  • Create a game or join using a game link
  • Wait for 2-4 players to join
  • Drag tiles from your rack to the board
  • Form valid Shavian words
  • Submit your move to score points

Game Rules

  • Standard Scrabble rules apply
  • 15×15 board with bonus squares (DL, TL, DW, TW)
  • Customizable rack size (default 9 tiles)
  • First word must cover the center square
  • All words must connect to existing tiles
  • 50 point bonus for using all tiles in your rack

Tile Modes

  • Rotation (default): 15 letter pairs share flippable tiles (27 types). Click to rotate.
  • Standard: All 42 Shavian letters as separate tiles.
  • Extended: Either mode can add yea and oevre — rare high-value letters replacing err (𐑺) and air (𐑻).

AI Opponents

Add bot players at four difficulty levels. Bots can be added before or during a game.

Endgame Scoring

Casual Rules (2-4 players):

  • Game ends when one player uses all tiles and the bag is empty
  • Or when six consecutive scoreless turns occur
  • Player who went out gets sum of opponents' remaining tiles added
  • Other players subtract their remaining tile values

Tournament Rules (2 players):

  • Same endgame conditions
  • Winner gets 2× opponent's remaining tiles (simpler math, same result)

Shavian Alphabet

This game uses the Shavian alphabet, designed by Kingsley Read. Compound letters (𐑽, 𐑼, 𐑸, 𐑹, 𐑾, 𐑿) are split into their constituent letters for gameplay.
