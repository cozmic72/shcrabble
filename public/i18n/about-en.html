About Shcrabble

Shcrabble is a multiplayer Scrabble game designed for the Shavian alphabet.

How to Play

  • Create a game or join using a game link
  • Wait for 2-4 players to join
  • Drag tiles from your rack to the board
  • Form valid Shavian words
  • Submit your move to score points

Game Rules

  • Standard Scrabble rules apply
  • 15×15 board with bonus squares
  • 7 tiles per player
  • First word must cover the center square
  • All words must connect to existing tiles
  • 50 point bonus for using all 7 tiles

Shavian Alphabet

This game uses the Shavian alphabet, designed by Kingsley Read. Compound letters (𐑽, 𐑼, 𐑸, 𐑹, 𐑾, 𐑿) are split into their constituent letters for gameplay.
