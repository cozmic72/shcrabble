Welcome to Shcrabble!

This is a Scrabble game for the Shavian alphabet. Before you start playing, here's an important note about compound letters:

Compound Letters

In standard Shavian, there are 6 compound letters that represent two sounds:

Compound Split Into Represents
𐑽 𐑦𐑩𐑮 as in "hear"
𐑼 𐑩𐑮 as in "letter"
𐑸 𐑭𐑮 as in "car"
𐑹 𐑷𐑮 as in "nor"
𐑾 𐑦𐑩 as in "happy"
𐑿 𐑘𐑵 as in "use"

Important: In Shcrabble, these compounds are split into their constituent letters. You'll use the separate letters (shown in the middle column) to spell words that would normally use compounds.

For example, to spell "𐑣𐑽" (hear), you would place: 𐑣 𐑦 𐑩 𐑮

Ready to Play?

Drag tiles from your rack to the board, form valid Shavian words, and score points!
