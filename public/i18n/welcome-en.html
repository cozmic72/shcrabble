Welcome to Shcrabble!

Shcrabble uses rotation tiles by default — many Shavian letters are rotationally symmetric pairs that share a single tile. Click to flip!

𐑐 ⟷ 𐑚
𐑑 ⟷ 𐑛
𐑒 ⟷ 𐑜
𐑤 ⟷ 𐑮

Each side has its own point value — the harder letter scores more!

Compound letters (𐑼, 𐑸, etc.) are split into their parts.
