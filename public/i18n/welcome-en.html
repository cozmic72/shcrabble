Rotation Tiles

In rotation mode (the default), some tiles can be flipped. Each side scores differently:

𐑖5 ⟷ 𐑠10

Click a tile in your rack to flip it.

15 letter pairs share tiles, giving 27 tile types from the 42-letter alphabet. The harder letter scores more!

How to Play

Form valid Shavian words on the board, then submit your move to score.

  • Drag & drop tiles from your rack onto the board
  • Double-click a rack tile to auto-place it
  • Keyboard: number keys select tiles, arrows move the cursor, space places

All words formed (including cross-words) must be in the dictionary. Click Submit Move to score, or Recall to take tiles back.
